About Me

Hi, I’m Anirudh K N, a passionate and self-taught MERN Stack Developer with a strong interest in building modern, user-friendly web applications.

My journey into tech started with curiosity and dedication, and over time I have developed solid skills in JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and frontend technologies. I enjoy turning ideas into real-world applications that solve problems and create meaningful user experiences.

Before transitioning into software development, I worked as a pharmacy assistant, which helped me build discipline, attention to detail, and strong work ethics. Today, I bring the same commitment into my development work.

I’m continuously learning and improving my skills every day—whether it's mastering backend architecture, working with databases, or building full-stack applications.

Apart from coding, I’m also interested in public speaking, self-improvement, and communication, which helps me collaborate better and present ideas effectively.

My goal is to grow as a professional developer, contribute to impactful projects, and be part of a team where I can learn, build, and innovate.
